About Grazioso Salvare

Transforming Lives Through Specialized Animal Rescue Training

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Grazioso Salvare (Italian for "Graceful Rescue") has pioneered innovative approaches to rescue animal training. Our mission began with a simple yet powerful idea: every shelter animal has the potential to become a hero.

Working closely with animal shelters across the country, we identify and select animals with exceptional potential for rescue work. Through our specialized training programs, these animals are transformed into highly skilled rescue partners, serving in various critical missions worldwide.

Our Mission

At Grazioso Salvare, our mission is twofold:

  • To give shelter animals a second chance at life through purposeful training and meaningful work
  • To provide emergency response teams with highly trained animal partners for critical rescue operations

Our Approach

Our training methodology combines traditional animal training techniques with cutting-edge behavioral science. We focus on:

  • Individual assessment and personalized training plans
  • Positive reinforcement and trust-based training methods
  • Real-world scenario training in controlled environments
  • Ongoing support and certification programs

Training Facilities

Water Rescue Center

Our state-of-the-art aquatic training facility features controlled wave pools, rescue simulation areas, and specialized equipment for water rescue training.

Wilderness Training Ground

Featuring varied terrain and climate-controlled environments, our wilderness facility prepares animals for mountain and forest rescue operations.
