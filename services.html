Specialized Training for Life-Saving Missions

Water Rescue Training

Specialized training for maritime and aquatic rescue operations.

Program Features

  • Advanced swimming and water navigation
  • Maritime search and rescue techniques
  • Beach patrol and lifeguard support
  • Underwater object retrieval
  • Boat-based rescue operations

Training Process

Our comprehensive water rescue program spans 16 weeks, progressing from basic water comfort to advanced rescue scenarios. Training takes place in our state-of-the-art aquatic facility featuring controlled wave pools and rescue simulation areas.
